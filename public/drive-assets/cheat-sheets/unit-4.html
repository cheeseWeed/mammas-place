Utah alcohol BAC (lowest in U.S.)

  • Adult (21+) limit: 0.05.
  • Under 21 limit: 0.00 (zero tolerance). No alcohol in circulatory system.

Under 21 alcohol law

  • 3 illegal acts: purchase, possess, OR drink alcohol.
  • Not-a-Drop Act: under 21 + any measurable alcohol while driving = privileges denied 6 months (1st offense).
  • First DUI under 21 = 3-year Ignition Interlock restriction.

Implied Consent

  • By driving in Utah you have already agreed to chemical testing (breath / blood / urine / oral fluids) if law enforcement requests.
  • Refusal = its own penalty (license action), separate from DUI.

Impairing drugs

  • Impairing drugs include ALL 3 categories: illegal, over-the-counter, AND prescription.
  • "My doctor prescribed it" is NOT a defense if you are impaired.

Handheld wireless ban (HWC)

  • While driving on a highway you may NOT: manually write / send / read texts, dial, access internet, view/record video, or enter data on a handheld device.
  • Hands-free voice / single-touch / one-button OK.

Under-18 phone exceptions only

  • Under 18 may use a handheld wireless device ONLY for:
  • 1. Medical emergency.
  • 2. Reporting a safety hazard.
  • 3. Reporting criminal activity.
