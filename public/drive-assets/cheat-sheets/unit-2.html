Pavement lines (color = direction)

Yellow separates OPPOSING directions (oncoming traffic).
White separates traffic going the SAME direction.

Yellow line passing

  • Broken (dashed) yellow on your side = passing PERMITTED.
  • Solid yellow on your side = passing PROHIBITED in your direction.

Following distance

  • Freeway: at least 2 seconds behind the car ahead.
  • Increase in rain, snow, fog, ice, heavy traffic, or behind big trucks.

Speed limits (default)

  • 20 mph in school zones (during recess, opening/closing, or flashing lights).
  • 25 mph in any business or residential area.
  • School zone with flashing lights = obey the slower school-zone limit.

Work zones

  • Speeding in a work zone: fine is at least DOUBLE the regular speeding fine. By law.

Parking distances (memorize)

  • 15 feet from a fire hydrant — no parking.

Merging

  • Zipper method: at a congested merge, use BOTH lanes until the merge point, then alternate yielding one car at a time into the single lane.
