Unit 3 Cheat Sheet: Rules of the Road

Utah Driver Handbook REV 3.2026 · pp. 50–60

▽

◇ / ▭
Orange diamond/rectangle = WORK ZONE

Sign colors

  • Red = STOP, YIELD, or PROHIBITION.
  • Yellow = general WARNING.
  • Orange = construction / work zone.

Traffic signals

  • Red light: complete stop before the intersection, behind stop line / crosswalk. Wait until green.
  • Green light: still yield to pedestrians in the crosswalk.
  • Lights out (dark): treat as 4-way STOP. Yield to vehicle on your right.

Right-of-way priority ladder

  • Trains beat everyone — including ambulances, fire engines, police, pedestrians, you.
  • Emergency vehicle (siren/lights): pull immediately to the right, STOP until it passes.
  • Turning left: yield to all oncoming traffic first.
  • 4-way stop, simultaneous arrivals: yield to the vehicle on your RIGHT.

Pedestrians

  • Yield to ALL pedestrians at any crosswalk — marked OR unmarked.
  • Pedestrian with a white cane or guide dog = full STOP.

School buses

  • Alternating flashing red lights = STOP immediately before reaching the bus.
  • 2-lane road: BOTH directions must stop.
  • Divided highway with median (4+ lanes): ONLY same-direction traffic must stop.

Railroad crossings

  • Flashing lights or bells = MUST STOP.
  • Never drive around or under a lowered gate.

Move Over Law

  • Approaching any stationary vehicle with flashing hazard lights: slow down AND change lanes to a non-adjacent lane when safe.
